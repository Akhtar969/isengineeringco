Our Story

I.S. Engineering Co. was founded with a simple vision: to provide India's industrial sector with the highest quality flanges, fittings, and pipes at competitive prices, backed by unmatched service.

Based in the heart of Mumbai's industrial zone at Kolsa Bunder, Darukhana — we have grown from a small trading firm to a respected manufacturer and supplier serving clients across India.

Our team of experienced engineers and procurement specialists ensures that every product we supply meets international standards and your exact specifications. Whether you need a single flange or bulk pipe supply for a large project, we deliver with the same commitment to quality.

Today, I.S. Engineering Co. is proud to serve clients in Oil & Gas, Petrochemical, Power Generation, Chemical processing, and EPC sectors with an ever-expanding product portfolio.

Product Catalogue

Precision Engineered Products

All products are manufactured to international standards including ANSI, BS, DIN, IS. Available in multiple grades, sizes, and pressure ratings.

🔩
Flanges
M.S. Slip-On Flanges
Mild steel slip-on flanges for general piping applications. Easy to install and weld.
  • Standard ANSI B16.5 / IS 6392
  • Size Range ½" – 36"
  • Pressure Rating 150# – 2500#
  • Material A105 / IS 2062
⭕
Flanges
M.S. Weld Neck Flanges
High-pressure welding neck flanges for critical piping systems with superior integrity.
  • Standard ANSI B16.5
  • Size Range ½" – 24"
  • Pressure Rating 150# – 2500#
  • Material A105 / Carbon Steel
🔵
Flanges
M.S. Blind Flanges
Blind flanges for closing pipe ends and pressure vessel openings. Stock in all sizes.
  • Standard ANSI B16.5 / BS 4504
  • Size Range ½" – 48"
  • Pressure Rating 150# – 1500#
  • Material Carbon Steel
✨
Flanges
S.S. Flanges
Stainless steel flanges in SS 304, SS 316 & Duplex grades for corrosive environments.
  • Grade SS 304 / SS 316 / Duplex
  • Type Slip-On, WN, Blind, SO
  • Standard ANSI / DIN / BS
  • Finish Natural / Pickled
🔧
Fittings
Buttweld Elbows
90° and 45° elbows in short and long radius. MS and SS grades available in all schedules.
  • Angle 45° / 90° / 180°
  • Type LR / SR
  • Standard ASME B16.9
  • Size Range ½" – 24"
🔱
Fittings
Equal & Reducing Tees
Equal and reducing tees for branching pipe runs in process and utility piping.
  • Type Equal / Reducing
  • Standard ASME B16.9
  • Material MS / SS / Alloy
  • Size Range ½" – 24"
🔺
Fittings
Concentric & Eccentric Reducers
Pipe reducers for connecting pipes of different diameters while maintaining flow direction.
  • Type Concentric / Eccentric
  • Standard ASME B16.9
  • Material Carbon / SS
  • Size Range ¾" – 24"
🪈
Pipes
ERW / Seamless Pipes
Electric resistance welded and seamless pipes for structural, mechanical, and process applications.
  • Type ERW / Seamless
  • Standard ASTM A53 / A106 / IS 1239
  • Size ½" – 12" NB
  • Schedule Sch 40 / 80 / 160
⚪
Pipes
S.S. Pipes
Stainless steel pipes for high-purity, corrosion-resistant applications in food, pharma, and chemical industries.
  • Grade SS 304 / 316 / 316L
  • Type Welded / Seamless
  • Standard ASTM A312
  • Finish 2B / No.4 / Mirror
